# Bank Marketing Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the Bank Marketing dataset and presents business insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard. The objective is to identify the customer segments and financial factors that influence term deposit subscriptions.

## Dataset Information
- Source: UCI Bank Marketing Dataset
- Total Records: 11,162
- Total Columns: 17
- Target Variable: deposit (yes/no)

## Key Insights
- Overall conversion rate is 47.38%.
- Customers with higher account balances are more likely to subscribe.
- Students and retired customers have the highest conversion rates.
- Customers without personal or housing loans convert significantly better.
- Tertiary-educated customers show stronger subscription rates.

## Business Recommendations
- Target high-balance customers.
- Focus on retired and student segments.
- Prioritize customers without active loans.
- Use customer segmentation to optimize marketing campaigns.
